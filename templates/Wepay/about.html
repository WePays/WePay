What is WePay?
          WePay is a web application for those who need to coordinate paying for a group. WePay can help by charging or splitting bills, which makes it easier to collect money. In this app, users can select to split a bill equally or separate a bill for each person. Then you can coordinate intra-group, making payment more accurate and easier.
